Solapur

Solapur, a typical Maharashtrian village renowned for temples and valiant fort, provides a calm and quiet ambience to the tourists from all parts of the world. It is the largest city in Southern Maharashtra and the 4th largest city in the entire Maharashtra. The city, which lies in the border of Karnataka, is the 30th most populous city in the country.
